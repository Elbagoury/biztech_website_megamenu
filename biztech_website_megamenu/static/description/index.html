Display Your Offerings Effectively

The 'Advance Mega Menu' app allows you to categorize the brands of your store and helps you show it in a mega menu. You can have as many mega menus you want in the website. You can have multiple columns in it as decided by you in back end. You can also add a slider in your armory to display latest and quick selling products of the store with Odoo website mega menu. The look of the menu will get creative edge with different background images and background colors.

Basic Features:

Give a unique label to the mega menu.
Redirection of pages from the mega Menu itself.
Multiple columns for multiple mega menus.
Innovative movable slider for quick view.
Mega menu footer to display message to customers.
Multiple Categories & Sub Categories.

Multiple Mega Menus

You can have multiple Mega Menus in odoo website with Odoo mega menu plugin. And you can also have different range of columns in mega menu from 2 Columns to 4 Columns. You can also change order of the Mega Menus from back end and display it as you want.

Mega Menu Customization

You can customize your mega menu in multiple ways. You can customize following things of a mega menu:

Mega Menu image
Mega Menu Background with color or image
Mega Menu Footer
Category & Sub Category Fonts

Slider Customization

You can add a slider in your Mega Menu as well. You can define name of the slider as well as position of the slider.

Multiple Categories & Sub Categories

You can add multiple categories and sub categories in the Mega Menu. You just have to define categories and sub categories accordingly for displaying in menu. The product displayed here will be shown in the slider.
